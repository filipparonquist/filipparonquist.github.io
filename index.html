Filippa Ronquist

I am a philosopher, working on topics in moral, political and legal philosophy. Currently, I am finishing my PhD in philosophy at UCL. I am spending the academic year 2025-2026 as a Sweden-America Fellow at UC Berkeley.

I did my MPhil in philosophy at UCL, and my BA in politics, philosophy and economics (PPE) at Oxford.
